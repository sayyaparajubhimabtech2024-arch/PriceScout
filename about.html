About PriceScout

At PriceScout, our mission is simple — to help shoppers make informed decisions effortlessly. Founded in 2025, our platform scans prices from leading e-commerce sites like Amazon, Flipkart, Reliance Digital, and Croma to ensure you always get the best deal.

Our Vision

To revolutionize online shopping by combining transparency, technology, and trust — enabling users to save both time and money while discovering smarter choices.

How It Works

1. Collect Data

Our engine collects live price data from multiple platforms in real-time.

2. Compare Instantly

We instantly compare the prices and highlight the cheapest deal available for you.

3. Save Money

With advanced filters and updates, you can shop seamlessly and save.
